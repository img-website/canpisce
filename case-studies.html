Workplace Organization & 5S Implementation

India's Leading FMCG Manufacturing Unit

Challenges
  • Disorganized shop floor, causing frequent material search and loss of productive time.
  • Unsafe work practices and cluttered workspace are impacting efficiency.
  • Lack of ownership and accountability for workplace standards.
Our Approach
  • Conducted 5S training and workshops across all departments.
  • Created area-wise 5S zones with defined responsibilities.
  • Designed visual management boards and standard checklists.
  • Established a daily audit and review mechanism for sustenance.
Outcomes
  • 40% reduction in search and movement time
  • Improved workplace safety and visual control
  • Enhanced employee ownership and productivity mindset

ERP Process Standardization & Implementation

India's Leading HoReCa and QSR segment

Challenges
  • Lack of standardization across Order to Cash (OtC), Procure to Pay (PtP), and Make to Dispatch (MtD) processes
  • Inefficient manual flow causes delays and manual dependencies
  • Limited visibility into inventory and production planning
  • Disjointed data across departments, leading to poor decision-making
Our Approach
  • Mapped and standardized core processes—OtC, PtP, and MtD—aligned with ERP workflows
  • Designed and implemented Demand-Driven MRP (DDMRP) with buffer and replenishment planning
  • Integrated dispatch planning with load building and ATP (Available-to-Promise) functionality
  • Conducted ideation workshops to drive user alignment and change management
Outcomes
  • Clean and reliable ERP system with better Master data visibility and inventory levels.
  • 48% improvement in the Books vs Physical inventory
  • OTIF improved by 40% hence the faster customer service
  • Streamlined end-to-end process flow across departments

Yard Management System Implementation

India's Leading Multi-Modal Logistics Company

Challenges
  • Difficulty in locating containers due to manual tracking
  • High turnaround time for container movement and dispatch
  • Inefficient yard space utilization leading to congestion
  • Lack of real-time visibility of container status and location
Our Approach
  • Developed and deployed a customized Yard Management System (YMS)
  • Integrated container tracking with unique address-based search functionality
  • Enabled real-time dashboard for container visibility and yard space optimization
  • Established digital SOPs to streamline yard operations and reduce manual dependency
Outcomes
  • 60% reduction in container search time and traffic flow
  • Improved yard space utilization and traffic flow
  • Faster TAT (Turnaround Time) for trucks due to proper visibility of containers.
  • Enhanced operational visibility and decision-making efficiency

DDMRP & MPS Implementation in Business Central

India's Leading FMCG Manufacturing Company

Challenges
  • Frequent stockouts and excess inventory due to static planning parameters
  • Limited visibility on real demand and supply variations
  • Manual intervention in production and procurement planning
  • Inefficient replenishment and scheduling cycles impacting service levels
Our Approach
  • Implemented Demand Driven MRP (DDMRP) framework within Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central
  • Integrated Master Production Scheduling (MPS) with DDMRP for balanced short-term and long-term planning
  • Configured dynamic buffer management to ensure optimal stock levels and responsiveness
  • Enabled automated replenishment planning aligned with demand signals and lead times
  • Conducted cross-functional training to align planners, schedulers, and buyers on DDMRP principles
  • Deployed real-time dashboards for visibility into production, stock, and buffer health
Outcomes
  • 25% reduction in inventory while maintaining service levels
  • Improved production stability through synchronized MPS and DDMRP planning
  • Faster and more reliable replenishment and scheduling cycles
  • Enhanced visibility and agility in supply chain decision-making

Six Sigma Implementation using DMAIC Approach

India's Leading Laminate Manufacturing Company

Challenges
  • Frequent surface defects and quality rejections are impacting yield
  • Lack of data-driven analysis for process variations
  • Inconsistent production parameters across shifts
  • High rework and material wastage are affecting cost and delivery timelines
Our Approach
  • Adopted Six Sigma methodology using the DMAIC framework (Define–Measure–Analyze–Improve–Control)
  • Defined critical-to-quality (CTQ) parameters and established baseline performance
  • Conducted root cause analysis using Pareto charts, fishbone diagrams, and process capability studies
  • Implemented process improvements through optimized temperature, pressure, and curing parameters
  • Deployed control mechanisms and real-time quality dashboards to sustain improvements
Outcomes
  • Quality improved from 98% to 99.5%
  • Significant reduction in defects and rework
  • Improved process capability and yield consistency
  • Enhanced culture of data-driven decision-making and continuous improvement
