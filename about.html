About Us

Enabling organizations to achieve measurable business performance

At Canpisce Consulting, we enable organizations to achieve measurable business performance improvements through data-driven transformation, operational excellence, and digital integration. Our consulting approach is rooted in technical rigor, system thinking, and process optimization — ensuring every solution translates into quantifiable business outcomes.

We specialize in implementing Lean, Six Sigma, DDMRP, and ERP-integrated planning systems that enhance flow, reduce waste, and improve service levels across manufacturing and supply chain ecosystems. By leveraging analytics, process diagnostics, and performance benchmarking, we design interventions that drive step-change improvements in efficiency, quality, and profitability.

Our expertise spans FMCG, Multi-Modal Logistics, QSR, Packaging, Digitalisation, where we have delivered tangible results such as:

Efficiency gains of 15–30% through process re-engineering

Quality improvement from 98% to 99.5% using Six Sigma (DMAIC approach)

Lead time reduction through DDMRP and pull-based replenishment models

Integrated ERP design aligning Order-to-Cash, Procure-to-Pay, and Make-to-Dispatch processes

Why Choose Us

We combine strategic design with shop-floor execution, ensuring sustainable capability development within client teams. Our goal is not just to recommend best practices but to institutionalize high-performance systems that enable real-time visibility, agility, and continuous improvement.

At Canpisce Consulting, transformation is not a presentation — it’s an implementation. We measure success by the results our clients achieve: reduced cycle times, optimized inventory, enhanced throughput, and improved bottom-line performance.
